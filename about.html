About Campus Convoz


Campus Convoz is a project by Sarah Dapul-Weberman and Scott Khamphoune for CS376: Research Topics in Human-Computer Interaction, taught by Michael Bernstein in Spring 2013. Right now, Campus Convoz has only launched to Stanford University.

Why Campus Convoz?
Well, campusconvos.com was taken. So was campusconversations.com, campuschat.com, campusanonymous.com, and basically everything else we tried.
